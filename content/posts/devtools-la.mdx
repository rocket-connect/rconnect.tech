From the creators of DevTools Asia, we bring the community "DevTools America" with the first event in Los Angeles. This event will be hosted by [Slalom](https://www.slalom.com/) at their office in Los Angeles on the 11th of July 2024. We will have a range of speakers from the local community and beyond. The event will be free to attend and will be hosted on [Guild](https://guild.host/events/devtools-la-001-at-slalom-k7xsb4). If you would like to speak at this event, please [Contact Us](/contact).

## Sign Up
- It's free to attend

To sign up for this event, please visit the [Guild Event Page](https://guild.host/events/devtools-la-001-at-slalom-k7xsb4).


## Event Details

- Date: 11th of July 2024
- Time: 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM
- Location: [Slalom Los Angeles](https://maps.app.goo.gl/iLxQrQRJK8QKfUCY6)
- Price: Free
