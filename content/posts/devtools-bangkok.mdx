On the 19th of March 2024, [Sevenpeaks](https://sevenpeakssoftware.com/), [HubQL](https://www.hubql.com/), and [Rocket Connect](https://www.rconnect.tech) delivered an insightful event titled "Supercharge: 10x Your Developer Productivity with GenAI & HubQL." The event covered a wide range of topics, including modern dev tools, Generative AI, and productivity enhancements.

The event was hosted on [Guild](https://guild.host/) and uploaded to YouTube.
