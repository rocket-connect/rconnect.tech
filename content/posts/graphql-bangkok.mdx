On the 7th of March 2024, [Agoda](https://www.agoda.com/), [HubQL](https://www.hubql.com/), and [Rocket Connect](https://www.rconnect.tech) delivered an insightful event titled "GraphQL Bangkok 13.0 Meetup." The event had a variety of topics, including working with Graphs and GraphQL, understanding GraphQL schemas with visualization, mocking GraphQL APIs, and managing GraphQL APIs.

The event was hosted on [Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/) and uploaded to YouTube.

1. [Meetup Event](https://www.meetup.com/graphql-bangkok/events/298478165/)

- Leon Nunes
