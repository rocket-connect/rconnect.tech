From the creators of [The 90 Members Club](https://www.linkedin.com/company/the-90/), the London developer community experienced "DevTool Founders" on the 26th of June at the University of London. This event featured a range of speakers, including keynote speakers, panel discussions, and a networking opportunity with tech founders in London. The event was free to attend and hosted on Guild. For those interested in speaking at future events, please [Contact Us](https://www.rconnect.tech/contact).

This event was a collaboration between [Bellwether Industries](https://www.bams-tech.com/technologies/) and [Rocket Connect](https://www.rconnect.tech/).

## Talks

### Startup Science Masterclass

- [Taz Singh](https://twitter.com/tazsingh)

Taz from [guild.host](https://guild.host/) will provide an interactive deep-dive into hard lessons learned over his 25-year career in tech, including unique management techniques, business modeling, and growth strategies for each phase of a company. There will be a live Q&A with the audience throughout. Listen to [this](https://podcasters.spotify.com/pod/show/the-hard-part/episodes/Taz-Singh-from-Guild-e2kbjhv) podcast with Taz for more.

### Single Sign-On (SSO): The Key to a Seamless User Experience

- [Fung Cheng](https://www.linkedin.com/in/lfcheng/)

Fung Cheng from [Skymakers](https://www.skymakers.co.uk/) will shed light on Single Sign-On (SSO) and its importance in creating a smooth user experience. In his talk, Fung Cheng will cover: Why SSO is a game-changer for businesses and users alike, different SSO implementations: Websites, Apps, App2App, etc, how to choose the right SSO approach for your specific needs, and a live demo.
