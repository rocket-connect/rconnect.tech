From the creators of [The 90 Members Club](https://www.linkedin.com/company/the-90/), we bring the London developer community "DevTool Founders." This event will be hosted on the 26th of June at the University of London. We will have a range of speakers, including keynote speakers, panel discussions, and a networking opportunity with tech founders in London. The event will be free to attend and will be hosted on Guild. If you would like to speak at this event, please [Contact Us](https://www.rconnect.tech/contact).

This event is a collaboration between [Bellwether Industries](https://www.bams-tech.com/technologies/) and, [Rocket Connect](https://www.rconnect.tech/).

## Signup
- It's free to attend

To sign up for this event, please [visit the Guild Event Page](https://guild.host/events/devtool-founders-by-tbsq0p).
