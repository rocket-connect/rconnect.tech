## Featured Speakers

### Neo4j MCP Server Deep Dive

**Dan Starns** - Founder & CTO, Rocket Connect  
[LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/danielstarns/)

Dan will demonstrate the powerful Neo4j MCP (Model Context Protocol) server, showing how graph databases can seamlessly integrate with AI models to provide rich, contextual data access. As a former core contributor to Neo4j and current advocate for graph database adoption, Dan brings deep technical insights into practical AI integration patterns.

### MCP Overview

**Tobias Meixner** - Co-founder, HubQL  
[LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/meixnertobias/)

Tobias will provide a comprehensive overview of Model Context Protocol, exploring how this emerging standard is transforming AI-data interactions. Learn about the ecosystem, implementation patterns, and why MCP is becoming essential for modern AI applications.

### Graph Database Trends & Insights

**Special Guest from Neo4j**

Latest developments in graph technology and emerging trends shaping the future of connected data. Get insights into Neo4j's roadmap and the evolving landscape of graph database applications.

## Why Graph Databases + AI Matter

The intersection of graph databases and AI represents one of the most exciting frontiers in modern software development. Graph databases excel at capturing complex relationships and providing contextual data that AI models need for accurate, relevant responses. With the emergence of Model Context Protocol, integrating these technologies has never been more straightforward.
