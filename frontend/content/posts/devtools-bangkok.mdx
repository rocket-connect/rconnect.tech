On the 19th of March 2024, Seven Peaks, HubQL, and Rocket Connect delivered an insightful event titled "Supercharge: 10x Your Developer Productivity with GenAI & HubQL." The event coverd a wide range of topics, including modern dev tools, Generative AI, and productivity enhancements.

The event was hosted on guild.host and uploaded to YouTube.
